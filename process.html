Our Process

From finding the bed to getting it home.

1

We Match You With a Bed

We either have the right bed or help find one that fits your truck. Once we know the bed, scope, timing, and delivery plan, we agree on pricing.

2

Deposit & Paint Code

We collect a $500 deposit and your paint code so we can reserve the bed and get the repair, paint, and scheduling process started.

3

Repair, Paint & Bedliner

We make needed repairs, paint the bed to match, and add a spray-in bedliner if you want one included before pickup, shipping, or install.

4

Pickup, Shipping, or Install

You can pick it up, arrange freight shipping and we’ll help load it, or schedule a same-day install so your truck leaves with the replacement bed on it.
