Out-of-State FAQ

Common questions before making the trip.

Do you install beds out of state?

No. Better Beds installs at our Dallas shop. We want the truck here so fitment, installation, and final details can be handled correctly.

Can I reserve a bed before driving?

Yes. Once the bed, price, paint plan, and timing are agreed on, we typically collect a deposit to reserve the bed and start the process.

Can I wait while it is installed?

Many scheduled installs are completed the same day once the bed is ready, but timing depends on the job. Confirm before travel.

Do you ship?

Customer-arranged freight may be possible, and we can help load the carrier, but shipping is not the main path because it can add a lot to the cost.
