Simple process

Send photos. Get options. Fix the truck.

  1. Tell us what you have. Year, make, model, bed length, color, and what kind of damage or replacement you need.
  2. Send clear photos. Wide shots, closeups of damage, tailgate, inside bed, and paint code if available.
  3. We recommend the best path. Repair, replace, paint, install — whatever makes the most sense for the truck and budget.
