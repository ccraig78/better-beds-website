Agent Intake Checklist

Collect this before asking for a free estimate.

More complete requests get better answers. Photos are especially important for repair, replacement, and collision damage decisions.

Customer and truck

  • Name and best phone/email
  • City and state
  • Year, make, model, and trim if known
  • Bed length/style: short, standard, long, dually, not sure
  • Paint code or color if known

Need and timing

  • Repair, replacement, collision repair, paint, bedliner, install, or unsure
  • What happened / what problem needs solved
  • Insurance or self-pay
  • Desired timing and whether customer can come to Dallas
  • Whether the truck is drivable

Photos to request

  • Full side and rear views of the truck
  • Close-ups of rust, dents, collision damage, or bedside damage
  • Inside bed floor, wheel wells, and corners if relevant
  • Paint code sticker if available
  • Any replacement bed being considered
